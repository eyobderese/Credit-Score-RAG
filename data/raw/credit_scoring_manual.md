## 1. Credit Score Ranges and Risk Categories

### 1.1 Score Classification

Our credit scoring system uses a range of 300-850. Applicants are categorized as follows:

| Credit Score Range | Risk Category | Default Risk | Approval Rate |
|-------------------|---------------|--------------|---------------|
| 800-850 | Exceptional | <1% | 98% |
| 740-799 | Very Good | 1-2% | 95% |
| 670-739 | Good | 3-5% | 85% |
| 580-669 | Fair | 8-15% | 50% |
| 300-579 | Poor | >20% | 10% |

### 1.2 Minimum Credit Score Requirements by Product

**Personal Loans:**
- Minimum credit score: 580
- Preferred score: 670 or higher
- Exception threshold: 550 (requires manual underwriting)

**Mortgage Loans:**
- Conventional: Minimum 620
- FHA: Minimum 580 (with 3.5% down payment)
- FHA: Minimum 500 (with 10% down payment)
- Jumbo loans: Minimum 700

**Business Loans:**
- Small business (<$100K): Minimum 650
- Medium business ($100K-$500K): Minimum 680
- Large business (>$500K): Minimum 700

**Auto Loans:**
- New vehicles: Minimum 600
- Used vehicles: Minimum 580
- Luxury vehicles: Minimum 680

### 2.1 Personal Loan Rates (APR)

| Credit Score | Rate Range | Typical Rate |

| 800+ | 6.99% - 8.99% | 7.49% |

| 670-739 | 11.99% - 15.99% | 13.49% |

### 2.2 Mortgage Rates (30-Year Fixed)

- Exceptional credit (800+): Base rate + 0.00%

- Fair (620-669): Base rate + 1.00%

## 3. Credit History Requirements

### 3.1 Minimum Account Age
- At least 2 credit accounts open for 12+ months
- Average account age: 24 months (preferred)
- Minimum credit history length: 12 months

### 3.2 Recent Inquiries
- Hard inquiries in last 6 months: Maximum 3
- Hard inquiries in last 12 months: Maximum 6
- Multiple inquiries for same loan type within 14 days count as 1

### 3.3 Derogatory Marks

**Automatic Declination:**
- Active bankruptcy (Chapter 7 or 13)
- Foreclosure within 36 months
- Repossession within 24 months

**Waiting Periods After Resolution:**
- Bankruptcy discharge: 24 months (Chapter 13), 48 months (Chapter 7)
- Foreclosure completion: 36 months
- Short sale: 24 months
- Deed in lieu: 24 months

**Acceptable with Conditions:**
- Collections <$500 (no payment required)
- Collections $500-$2,000 (payment plan acceptable)
- Collections >$2,000 (must be paid or settled)
- Medical collections: Ignored if <$1,000

## 4. Credit Utilization Standards

### 4.1 Revolving Credit Utilization

| Utilization Rate | Impact | Required for Approval |
|------------------|--------|----------------------|
| 0-10% | Excellent | Preferred |
| 11-30% | Good | Acceptable |
| 31-50% | Fair | Acceptable with compensating factors |
| 51-75% | Poor | Requires explanation |
| 76-100% | Very Poor | Generally declined |

**Policy:** Applicants with >50% utilization must have compensating factors (high income, low DTI, excellent payment history).

### 4.2 Individual Account Utilization
- No single account should exceed 80% utilization
- Maxed out accounts (>95%): Maximum 1 allowed

---

## 5. Payment History Requirements

### 5.1 Late Payment Thresholds

**Last 12 Months:**
- 30-day late: Maximum 1 allowed
- 60-day late: Not permitted
- 90+ day late: Automatic decline

**Last 24 Months:**
- 30-day late: Maximum 2 allowed
- 60-day late: Maximum 1 allowed
- 90+ day late: Maximum 1 allowed (must be >18 months old)

### 5.2 Payment History Score Weight
- 0 late payments (24 months): 100% score
- 1 late payment (30 days): -15 points
- 2 late payments (30 days): -25 points
- 1 late payment (60 days): -35 points
- 1 late payment (90+ days): -50 points

---

## 6. Special Circumstances

### 6.1 First-Time Borrowers
- Credit score requirement reduced by 20 points
- Requires co-signer if score <620
- Minimum down payment: 15%

### 6.2 Credit Builder Programs
- Minimum score: 550
- Maximum loan amount: $5,000
- Required secured deposit: 25% of loan amount
- APR: Fixed at 18.99%

### 6.3 Military and Veterans
- Score requirement reduced by 30 points for VA loans
- Active duty: Additional 0.25% rate discount
- Deployment exceptions for late payments in last 12 months

---

## 7. Score Adjustments and Overlays

### 7.1 Compensating Factors (Add Points)
- Income >150% of area median: +15 points
- Debt-to-Income <25%: +10 points
- Down payment >20%: +10 points
- Liquid reserves >6 months: +15 points
- Employment tenure >5 years: +5 points

### 7.2 Risk Factors (Subtract Points)
- Self-employed: -10 points
- Declining income trend: -15 points
- Job change in last 6 months: -10 points
- High-risk industry: -20 points
- Cash-out refinance: -5 points
