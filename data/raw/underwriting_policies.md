## 1. Documentation Requirements

### 1.1 Credit Documentation

**Required for All Applications:**
- Tri-merge credit report (Equifax, Experian, TransUnion)
- Credit report must be <120 days old at funding
- Applicant written explanation for:
  - Credit scores <680
  - Credit inquiries in last 90 days
  - Collections >$500
  - Any derogatory marks in last 24 months

### 1.2 Identity Verification

**Primary Documents (Need 1):**
- Valid government-issued photo ID (driver's license, passport, state ID)
- ID must not expire within 90 days of application

**Secondary Verification (Need 1):**
- Recent utility bill (<60 days)
- Bank statement showing address
- Lease agreement or mortgage statement

**Additional Requirements:**
- SSN verification via credit report
- For loan amounts >$100K: Additional photo ID or notarized signature

### 1.3 Income Documentation by Source

**W-2 Employees:**
- Most recent 2 years W-2 forms
- Most recent 30 days pay stubs
- Verbal VOE (verification of employment)
- Written VOE for loans >$250K

**Self-Employed:**
- Personal 1040 returns (2 years) with all schedules
- Business returns (2 years): 1120, 1120S, 1065, Schedule C
- Year-to-date P&L (signed by applicant)
- CPA letter for loans >$250K
- Business license/DBA registration

**Retired:**
- Social Security award letter
- Pension/401K distribution letter
- Most recent bank statements showing deposits (3 months)

**Rental Income:**
- Lease agreements (all properties)
- Schedule E from tax returns (2 years)
- Property tax bills
- Mortgage statements for rental properties

### 1.4 Asset Documentation

**Bank Accounts:**
- Most recent 2 months statements
- All pages required (including blank pages with page numbers)
- Large deposits (>50% monthly income) must be sourced
- Statements must show account holder name and account number

**Retirement Accounts:**
- Most recent quarterly statement
- If using for reserves: Vesting schedule required

**Investments:**
- Most recent quarterly statement
- 70% of current value used for qualification
- Letter from broker if liquidation required

**Gift Funds:**
- Gift letter (signed by donor and donee)
- Donor bank statement showing withdrawal
- Donee bank statement showing deposit
- Acceptable donors: Family members, employer (max $1000)
- Minimum seasoning after deposit: 60 days OR proof of transfer

## 2. Property Documentation

### 2.1 Appraisal Requirements

**Standard Requirements:**
- Licensed/certified appraiser
- Uniform Residential Appraisal Report (Form 1004)
- Comparable sales within 1 mile and 6 months
- Minimum 3 comparable properties

**Appraiser Qualifications:**
- State licensed or certified
- Not related to buyer, seller, or real estate agent
- Experience with property type and geographic area

**Review Requirements:**
| Loan Amount | Review Type |
|-------------|-------------|
| <$250K | Desktop review |
| $250K-$500K | Field review if value questioned |
| >$500K | Mandatory field review or second appraisal |

**Red Flags Requiring Second Appraisal:**
- Value >10% above comparable sales
- Declining market conditions
- Unique/special use property
- Rural location with limited comparables

### 2.2 Title and Insurance

**Title Search:**
- Minimum 30-year title search
- Title insurance policy required
- Lender coverage equal to loan amount
- Owner's policy recommended (not required)

**Property Insurance:**
- Hazard insurance equal to lesser of loan amount or replacement cost
- Mortgagee clause naming lender
- Proof of payment (first year prepaid at closing)
- Continuous coverage verified annually

**Additional Insurance by Property Type:**
- Flood zone: Flood insurance (coverage â‰¥ loan amount or max available)
- Condominiums: HO-6 policy plus master policy review
- Investment property: Loss of rents coverage recommended

### 2.3 Property Condition Requirements

**Minimum Standards:**
- Property must be safe, sound, and structurally secure
- All appliances included in sale must be operational
- Heating/cooling systems operational
- No health or safety hazards
- No adverse environmental conditions

**Repairs Required:**
- Defective roof
- Faulty electrical, plumbing, or HVAC
- Structural damage
- Pest infestation evidence
- Peeling/chipping paint (pre-1978 construction)

**Maximum Repair Threshold:**
- Repairs <$2,500: Seller can complete before closing
- Repairs $2,500-$10,000: Escrow holdback allowed (150% of cost)
- Repairs >$10,000: Must be completed before closing with re-inspection

## 3. Loan Structure Requirements

### 3.1 Term Limits by Product

**Mortgages:**
- Maximum term: 360 months (30 years)
- Minimum term: 60 months (5 years)
- Standard terms: 15, 20, 30 years

**Personal Loans:**
- Maximum term: 84 months (7 years)
- Minimum term: 12 months
- Standard terms: 36, 60, 84 months

**Auto Loans:**
- New vehicles: Maximum 72 months
- Used vehicles (0-5 years): Maximum 66 months
- Used vehicles (6-10 years): Maximum 48 months
- Classic/collector vehicles: Case-by-case approval

**Business Loans:**
- Equipment financing: Maximum 84 months
- Working capital: Maximum 60 months
- Commercial real estate: Maximum 300 months (25 years)

### 3.2 Loan Amount Limits

**Personal Loans:**
- Minimum: $1,000
- Maximum: $50,000 (unsecured)
- Maximum: $100,000 (secured)

**Mortgage Loans:**
- Conventional conforming: $766,550 (2026 limit, single unit)
- Jumbo: $766,551 to $3,000,000
- Super jumbo (>$3M): Chief Credit Officer approval required

**Business Loans:**
- Minimum: $10,000
- Maximum: $500,000 (delegated authority)
- >$500,000: Credit committee approval required

### 3.3 Rate Lock Policies

**Lock Period Options:**
- 15 days: Base rate - 0.125%
- 30 days: Base rate (no adjustment)
- 45 days: Base rate + 0.125%
- 60 days: Base rate + 0.250%

**Lock Extension:**
- First 15 days: 0.125% per extension
- Extensions beyond 15 days: 0.25% per 15 days
- Maximum lock period (including extensions): 90 days

**Float-Down Option:**
- Available if rate decreases by >0.25% during lock
- Fee: 0.25% of loan amount
- Must be elected within 5 days of rate change

## 4. Automated Underwriting Systems

### 4.1 DU/LP Approval Levels

**Accept/Approve:**
- Proceed with standard documentation
- No manual underwriting required
- Conditional approval issued

**Refer with Caution:**
- Manual underwrite required
- Detailed documentation needed
- Compensating factors must be demonstrated
- Senior underwriter review required

**Refer/Ineligible:**
- Does not meet automated guidelines
- Manual underwrite with enhanced documentation
- Manager approval required
- May require guideline exceptions

### 4.2 Manual Underwriting Criteria

**When Required:**
- Credit score <680
- Non-traditional credit
- Self-employed <2 years
- DTI >43%
- Significant derogatory credit
- Complex income sources

**Additional Documentation Requirements:**
- 12 months payment history on all debts
- Rental verification (12 months)
- Utility payment history
- Non-traditional credit references (if applicable)

### 4.3 Non-Traditional Credit

**Acceptable Sources (Minimum 3 Required):**
- Rent payments (verified by landlord or 12 months cancelled checks)
- Utility payments (electric, gas, water, cable/internet)
- Insurance payments (auto, life, health)
- Childcare payments
- School tuition payments

**Requirements:**
- 12-month payment history
- No payments >30 days late
- Documented via cancelled checks, bank statements, or provider verification

## 5. Special Program Guidelines

### 5.1 First-Time Homebuyer Program

**Eligibility:**
- No home ownership in past 3 years
- Completed homebuyer education course (8 hours minimum)
- Credit score minimum: 640
- Income limits: 115% of area median income

**Benefits:**
- Down payment: As low as 3%
- Down payment assistance: Available up to 3% of purchase price
- Rate reduction: 0.25% below standard rate
- Reduced closing costs

**Requirements:**
- Primary residence only
- Occupancy within 60 days of closing
- Cannot sell or refinance within 12 months without penalty
- Annual recertification (if down payment assistance received)

### 5.2 Energy-Efficient Mortgage (EEM)

**Purpose:**
- Finance energy efficiency improvements
- Up to 15% of appraised value added to loan amount

**Eligible Improvements:**
- Solar panels
- High-efficiency HVAC
- Insulation upgrades
- Energy-efficient windows/doors
- Energy Star appliances

**Requirements:**
- Energy audit required (cost can be financed)
- Projected savings must exceed payment increase
- Maximum addition to loan: $8,000
- Improvements must be completed within 90 days of closing

### 5.3 Physician Loan Program

**Eligibility:**
- MD, DO, DMD, DDS degree
- Within 5 years of residency completion
- Signed employment contract or proof of practice

**Benefits:**
- No PMI required up to 95% LTV
- Student loan payment: Use 0.5% of balance (instead of actual payment)
- No seasoned funds required
- Contract/future income may be used

**Requirements:**
- Minimum credit score: 700
- DTI maximum: 45%
- Loan amount: $500,000 to $1,500,000
- Primary residence only

## 6. Conditional Approval Standards

### 6.1 Standard Conditions

**Credit Conditions:**
- Updated credit report (if >90 days from original)
- Satisfactory explanation letters
- Payment of collections (if required)
- Subordination of liens (if applicable)

**Income Conditions:**
- Verbal VOE within 10 days of closing
- Final pay stub within 30 days of closing
- IRS 4506-C signed
- Award/benefit letters (if applicable)

**Asset Conditions:**
- Updated bank statements to within 60 days of closing
- Large deposit explanations
- Gift fund documentation
- Down payment verification

**Property Conditions:**
- Clear title report
- Satisfactory appraisal
- Homeowner's insurance binder
- HOA documentation (if applicable)
- Well/septic inspection (if applicable)

### 6.2 Prior to Document Conditions (PTD)

Must be cleared before loan documents prepared:
- Credit score verifications
- Income calculations
- Asset verifications
- Property valuation
- Title report preliminary review

### 6.3 Prior to Funding Conditions (PTF)

Must be cleared before loan funds disbursed:
- Final executed loan documents
- Funding authorization from all parties
- Clear to close issued
- Final walkthrough completed
- Hazard insurance policy active
- Final title policy issued

## 7. Quality Control Standards

### 7.1 Pre-Funding Review

**10% Random Sample:**
- Selected randomly via system
- Full file review by QC department
- Must be cleared before funding

**Discretionary Reviews:**
- First-time underwriter loans
- Exception approvals
- Loans with >2 conditions
- High-risk attributes present

### 7.2 Post-Funding Audit

**30-Day Post-Closing:**
- 15% of all loans reviewed
- Compliance verification
- Documentation completeness
- Underwriting accuracy

**Findings Categories:**
- Administrative: Minor documentation issues
- Underwriting: Judgment or calculation errors
- Compliance: Regulatory violations

**Corrective Actions:**
| Severity | Action |
|----------|--------|
| Minor | Underwriter coaching |
| Moderate | File review by manager, additional training |
| Severe | Removal of delegated authority, formal write-up |
| Critical | Suspension pending investigation |

### 7.3 Fraud Prevention

**Verification Requirements:**
- IRS 4506-C on all loans
- Tax transcript review for self-employed
- Direct employer verification (no applicant phone numbers)
- Bank statements verified via financial institution portal (when available)

**Red Flag Response:**
1. Document finding
2. Escalate to QC manager immediately
3. Enhanced verification on ALL documentation
4. Legal department notification (if fraud suspected)
5. Application withdrawal or funding hold

## 8. Closing and Funding

### 8.1 Clear to Close Criteria

**All Conditions Met:**
- All PTD conditions cleared
- All PTF conditions cleared
- Underwriter final sign-off
- QC review completed (if selected)

**Timing:**
- Clear to close issued minimum 72 hours before closing
- Documentation package sent to title company
- Funding authorization prepared

### 8.2 Closing Disclosure Review

**Required Review Period:**
- Initial CD: Provided 7 business days before closing
- Revised CD (APR change >0.125%): Additional 3 business day waiting period
- Minor corrections: No additional waiting period

**Tolerance Violations:**
- Zero tolerance items (>$0 increase): Cure required
- 10% cumulative tolerance exceeded: Cure required
- Cure methods: Seller credit, lender credit, fee reduction

### 8.3 Funding Authorization

**Required Approvals:**
| Loan Amount | Authorization Required |
|-------------|----------------------|
| <$150K | Underwriter |
| $150K-$500K | Senior underwriter |
| $500K-$1M | Underwriting manager |
| >$1M | Chief Credit Officer |

**Final Verifications:**
- Loan documents executed and notarized
- Closing figures balanced
- Title company wiring instructions verified
- OFAC check completed
- Final quality control sign-off
